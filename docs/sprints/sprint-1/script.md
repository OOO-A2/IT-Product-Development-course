### Critical Feedback Questions

Missing Functionality:
1. "Looking at the student progress dashboard, what functionality or features are missing from this prototype that you currently use in the spreadsheet?"
2. "When TAs grade assignments, what specific tools or information do they need that isn't shown here?"
3. "What administrative functions do you perform regularly that aren't represented in this prototype?"
4. "Are there any reporting or export capabilities you need that we haven't included?"

Workflow Gaps:
5. "Walk us through a complex grading scenario - what steps would be difficult with this interface?"
6. "What information do you need to see at a glance that would require multiple clicks in this design?"
7. "When multiple TAs work simultaneously, what coordination features are missing?"
